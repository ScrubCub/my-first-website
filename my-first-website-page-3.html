How to Setup Minecraft

How to install vanilla Minecraft

  1. Sign up for an account at Mojang
  2. Download the launcher
  3. Login to your account in launcher
  4. Choose version of Minecraft to play

How to get started with modded Minecraft

  1. Download Overwolf and Curseforge
  2. Login to your account in launcher
  3. Choose a modpack to install
  4. Wait for the Minecraft launcher to open and verify modpack profile
